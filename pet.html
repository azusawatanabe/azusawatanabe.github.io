Petit Souvenir: Branding and Web design

Petit Souvenir was a company founded by Maria Martha offering wedding invitations with very detailed and delicate design. To modernize the current brand, a new visual identity guidelines was created with a vintage look.

In the second phase, we designed a responsive website beginning by benchmarking, ideation phase, mock ups, development, review and launch.
