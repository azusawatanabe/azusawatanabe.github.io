iLove.e: Branding, UX/UI and advertising

A digital platform that offered fashion, beauty and lifestyle brands to users being a referential marketplace in the brazilian fashion's sector. The main goal was to change the target audience from a young woman to an adult public, without losing the look and feel already established.

Then, a deep process of designing a new visual identity and user experience had begun. The team dedicated a week for the Design sprint sessions including the CEO, developers and business owners.

All this data culminated in a whole new responsive website, information architecture and positioning. The new UX was developed based on the user's insights and the focus switched to show editorial content.

In the meantime, another important business activity was to provide advertising for partners. The strategy included sending CRM newsletters to increase qualified leads for fashion brands.
