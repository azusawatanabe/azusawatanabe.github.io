Free Flats Branding & User experience

AA new visual identity guide with a lot of creative freedom was developed for Free Flats. It is an online store focused on flat sandals that started its activities in 2016. The target audience of Free Flats is an urban woman who has a relaxed and cool fashion style. We have created everything from color guidelines, fonts, elements and a photo art direction. The project included the complete development of a fully responsive institutional website.
