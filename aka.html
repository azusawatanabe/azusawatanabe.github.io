AKASSI: A new branding and website

Andressa Akassi is a fashion consultant that requested a complete redesign to reach the right audience and broad her business.

The first step was to rethink all the imagery and look and feel of the brand to transmit elegancy and coziness.

The second step was to define the digital experience. Starting by user flows, reviews from the clients, low fidelity and high fidelity mockups, until reaching the final user experience.

The third step was the specifications, follow up with the development team and UX review.

Nowadays, AKASSI has a considerable social media fanbase and uses the new branding and website to meet her objectives.
